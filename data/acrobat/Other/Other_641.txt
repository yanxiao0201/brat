After the patient completed 3 months of warfarin therapy, an extended duration of anticoagulation was recommended, and risks and benefits were discussed with the patient. Despite the recommendation, she expressed concerns about her long-term risks of bleeding and requested that anticoagulation be discontinued. As an alternative, low-dose aspirin was initiated for secondary VTE prevention. She has experienced no further VTE events.
